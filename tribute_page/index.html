DOUGLAS NOEL ADAMS

"Chief Fantasist"

An English author, scriptwriter, essayist, humorist, satirist and dramatist

"He was one of the truly original writers and thinkers of our generation who should have had many years ahead of him. He was not only entertaining, but also stimulating and provoking: he was a unique thinker with a huge audience."
